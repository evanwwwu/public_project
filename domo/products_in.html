ALLY

ally trolly

The modular construction kit for the trolley consists of 7 different plastic parts, which are joined by corner profiles made from aluminium. Depending on which pieces are selected and at which point in the construction process, anything from a simple set of shelves to a piece of furniture with doors and/or drawers can be created. The result is a variety of useful trolleys for the bathroom, office, kitchen and other living and working areas. The high-quality, glossy ABS surface gives the furniture a touch of elegance and a modern feel, while the reflections in the surfaces form a lively contrast to the mechanical use of shapes of this product.

Material - ABS / Anodized aluminium / Wheels in zinc die-casting / Metal wire with powder coating
